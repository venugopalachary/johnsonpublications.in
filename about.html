Our Story

We started our journey with an aim to make learning more fun for children. Since our mission has started, we have launched many successful books and series like Little Champes, Little Stars, ITT-NEET Foundation Books, Pictorial Chart Books, Art & Craft Books, School Diary, Report Cards, Regional Language Text Books, Copy Writing Books and we are always ready to expand our range of quality books for every academic year.
